Financial Services Summit – Beyond Digital: New Chapter of Financial Services – An Invitation
 
 
 
 
 
 
AN INVITATION
20 October 2016 • Shangri-la at the Fort
 
Digital financial services have become the new normal. Going beyond the digital strategy is key to success (or survival) in the next two years. For this to happen, banks will need to be bold in re-architecting the existing infrastructure and systems, adopting an enterprise-wide digital transformation mindset and embracing collaboration with new partners. FIS and IDC Financial Insights invite you to join us on 20 October for a new perspective on digital so that you remain cycles ahead of your competition in this era of digital transformation. Mark your calendar to join us and stay tuned for more information.
 
 
 
Where and When
